About Us - Shikshan Jagruti Abhiyaan

Welcome to Shikshan Jagruti Abhiyaan, a beacon of educational empowerment and transformation in our community. Our mission is to illuminate the path of knowledge and bring education within reach for all, irrespective of age, background or circumstances.

Our Vision:

At Shikshan Jagruti Abhiyaan, we believe in the power of education to break down barriers and build brighter futures. Our vision is to create a society where every individual has access to quality education. We aim to:
1. Empower Through Education: We aspire to empower our students with knowledge and skills that will enable them to excel academically and in life.
2. Bridge the Gap: Shikshan Jagruti Abhiyaan is committed to bridging the educational divide in our community by providing accessible and affordable tuition classes.
3. Promote Academic Excellence: We strive to nurture a culture of academic excellence by fostering a love for learning, critical thinking and problem-solving.

Our Offerings:


1. Night Tuition Classes: We offer night tuition classes to accommodate students from all walks of life. Classes are from Magrib to Isha which is after the students daytime chaos so that every student can attend it. We are always here to support your educational journey.
2. Gujarati/English Medium Classes: Shikshan Jagruti Abhiyaan offers classes in both regional and English mediums. Our aim is to make quality education accessible to students from diverse linguistic backgrounds.
3. Computer Lab Sessions: In today's digital age, computer literacy is essential. We provide computer lab sessions to ensure that our students are well-equipped with the necessary digital skills for success in the modern world.
4. Standard 10th Preparation: We understand the importance of board exams, and our dedicated team of undergraduate girls and boys is committed to preparing our students for standard 10th board examinations. We provide comprehensive study materials, mock tests and personalized guidance to help our students excel in these crucial exams.

Our Team:


Shikshan Jagruti Abhiyaan is proud to have a team of dedicated and passionate undergraduate girls and boys who serve as tutors and mentors. They bring fresh perspectives, enthusiasm and a deep commitment to the educational growth of our students.

Join Our Mission:


We invite you to be a part of the Shikshan Jagruti Abhiyaan family, where education knows no boundaries. Whether you are a student seeking support or an individual passionate about education, together we can light up the path of knowledge and empower our community. Join us in our journey to make education a right, not a privilege, and together, let's write the story of educational transformation in our community.
